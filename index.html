About Me

I am a junior studying New Media Design at Rochester Institute of Technology. Through my twenty years of living in three different countries, going to different schools, and adapting to different cultures, the one thing that has been consistent is my love for the world, art, and design. I enjoy the process of problem solving and coming up with effective solutions for users. I believe that the person I have become is a collection of the books I've read, the people I've met, and the places I've been. After all, the world is only the stories we tell about it.
