Turtles All The Way Down

Objective

“The thing about a spiral is, if you follow it inward, it never actually ends. It just keeps tightening, infinitely.” -John Green

Recently, I read a book called "Turtles All the Way Down," this book allowed me to view the world from a different perspective. It speaks about mental illness, and how certain things in life are unmanageable. Yet, we get to choose how to react to situations, because life is a story told about you. Inspired by the above quote and Muhammed Salah's illustration style, I created a frame by frame animation to communicate my interpretation.
